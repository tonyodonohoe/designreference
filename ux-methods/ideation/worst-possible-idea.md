# Worst Possible Idea

The "Worst Possible Idea" is an ideation technique in UX design that encourages thinking of the most extreme or unconventional solutions to a problem. By exploring the absurd and challenging assumptions, designers can uncover innovative ideas and push boundaries, leading to unexpected insights and creative problem-solving.
