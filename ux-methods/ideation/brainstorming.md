# Brainstorming

Brainstorming is a classic and creative technique used in UX design to generate a wide range of ideas and solutions. It involves a group of individuals sharing thoughts and suggestions in a non-judgmental environment. Through collaborative thinking and ideation, brainstorming promotes innovation and helps in finding optimal design approaches for user-centric experiences.
