# Speed Dating

Speed dating is an ideation technique in UX design where designers present and gather feedback on their design concepts in short, structured sessions. Participants rotate between stations, providing quick insights and evaluations. Speed dating fosters rapid iteration, diverse perspectives, and helps validate design decisions in a time-efficient manner.
