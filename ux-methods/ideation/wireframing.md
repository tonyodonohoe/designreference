# Wireframing

Wireframing is a crucial step in UX design, where low-fidelity visual representations are created to outline the structure, layout, and functionality of a digital product or interface. It focuses on content and user interactions, helping designers plan and communicate the skeletal framework before adding visual details and aesthetics.
