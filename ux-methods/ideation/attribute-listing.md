# Attribute Listing

Attribute listing is a UX design technique that involves identifying and documenting key characteristics or attributes of a product or service.&#x20;

It helps designers analyse and prioritise elements like functionality, aesthetics, and usability, enabling them to make informed decisions and create user-centric designs that meet the desired objectives effectively.
