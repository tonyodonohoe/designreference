# Analytics Review

Analytics review refers to the evaluation and analysis of data collected from various sources, such as website analytics tools, to gain insights into user behaviour, website performance, and business metrics. It involves examining key performance indicators (KPIs), tracking data, and other relevant metrics to assess the effectiveness of digital marketing strategies, user experiences, and overall website or app performance.&#x20;

Analytics reviews help organisations understand user engagement, conversion rates, traffic sources, and other valuable information to make data-driven decisions. By analysing the data, organisations can identify areas of improvement, optimise marketing efforts, and enhance the user experience.&#x20;

The analytics review plays a crucial role in evaluating the success of digital initiatives, measuring the impact of marketing campaigns, and informing future strategies for growth and improvement.
