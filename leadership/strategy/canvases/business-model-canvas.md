# Business Model Canvas

Understanding how design impacts the overall business model is crucial. The Business Model Canvas helps articulate key elements like value propositions, customer segments, and revenue streams.&#x20;

By considering these factors, design managers ensure that design decisions not only meet user needs but also align with the business's value creation and capture mechanisms.&#x20;

For instance, a new design feature should be evaluated on how it enhances the product's value proposition and contributes to revenue growth or customer retention.
