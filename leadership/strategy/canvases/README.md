# Canvases

Canvases are visual frameworks used to structure and communicate complex ideas. They break down a concept into smaller, interrelated components, making it easier to analyse, brainstorm, and innovate.&#x20;

Examples include:

* **Business Model Canvas**\
  Used to visualise a business model
* **Value Proposition Canvas**\
  Which helps align value propositions with customer needs
* **Lean UX Canvas**\
  For rapid prototyping and iteration in product development
* **UX Research Canvas**\
  For planning and executing user research studies
