# PEST Analysis

PEST Analysis is a strategic tool used to assess the external factors that can influence a business's performance. It stands for Political, Economic, Social, and Technological factors. By analysing these elements, businesses can identify potential opportunities and threats, and develop strategies to mitigate risks and capitalise on favorable conditions.
